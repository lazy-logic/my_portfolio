CASE STUDY

CMS Development & Website Redesign

The website for Mole Machinery was originally built as a static HTML design with limited functionality and basic branding. While it served as an initial online presence, it lacked flexibility, scalability, and a modern visual identity.

To address these gaps, I redesigned and migrated the site to WordPress, creating a more dynamic, user-friendly, and professional platform. The new design incorporates updated branding, including a refreshed logo, and organizes content into clear sections that better showcase the company’s services and projects.

Summary of what I did

I transformed the website from a static HTML design to a dynamic WordPress platform, enhancing its visual appeal and functionality. Key updates included a modernized layout, improved navigation, and the integration of interactive elements.

Before
  • Static, plain layout with slider images.
  • Limited sections ("Why Us?", "Who We Are?", contact embedded).
  • Manual updates, minimal interactivity, basic visuals.

After
  • Modern, branded design with clear structure.
  • New logo and visual identity.
  • Full-featured pages: Services, Projects, About, Contact with dynamic forms.
  • Content easily managed through WordPress CMS.
  • Enhanced navigation—users can request quotes, email, or call directly.
  • A professional and scalable foundation for future content and functionality.

CASE STUDY

Branding

As part of my branding services, I worked with Ellen Foods to create a distinctive and professional identity that reflects their values and connects with their target audience. The process began with an initial consultation to understand their vision, audience, and brand personality.

From these insights, we developed a new logo design featuring their chosen color palette: #e07126, #f0c01a, and #b51e46. These colors were carefully selected to convey energy, quality, and trust, while creating a modern, eye-catching identity.

Beyond the logo, we designed product packaging that highlights the brand’s premium nature and ensures strong shelf presence. To help visualize the final outcome, we presented realistic mockups showing how the logo and packaging would appear across various mediums.

Highlights of the Branding Project

  • Initial brand consultation to align vision and goals.
  • Custom logo design with a unique and vibrant color palette.
  • Packaging design for Ellen Classic Peanuts, ensuring a strong brand presence.
  • Professional mockups to demonstrate real-world application.

SERVICES

UI/UX Design

Our UI/UX design services focus on creating intuitive, engaging, and visually appealing digital experiences. The goal is not only to design attractive interfaces, but also to ensure seamless interactions that meet user needs and align with business objectives.

We start every UI/UX project with research and discovery, studying the target audience, their pain points, and user journeys. Based on these insights, we craft wireframes, prototypes, and design systems that serve as the blueprint for the final product.

Finally, we deliver polished interface designs that balance aesthetics with functionality. Our designs are built to adapt across multiple devices, ensuring a consistent and user-friendly experience on desktop, tablet, and mobile.

Highlights of UI/UX Projects

  • Initial research and discovery sessions with stakeholders.
  • User journey mapping and wireframing to define structure.
  • Interactive prototypes to validate user flow and usability.
  • Final UI design with scalable design systems and style guides.
  • Responsive layouts ensuring cross-device consistency.
